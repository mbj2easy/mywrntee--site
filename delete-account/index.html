Delete Account & Data

MyWrntee · Warranty Tracker & Organizer · Developer: mbj2easy@gmail.com

You have the right to request complete deletion of your account and all associated data from MyWrntee. This page explains how to make that request and what data will be deleted.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to mbj2easy@gmail.com from the email address linked to your Google sign-in (or the email associated with your account).
  2. Use the subject line: "MyWrntee - Delete My Account & Data"
  3. In the email body, confirm that you want all your data permanently deleted.
  4. We will process your request and delete all associated data within 30 days.
  5. You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete.
Request Account Deletion

What Data Is Deleted

When your deletion request is processed, all of the following data associated with your account is permanently removed:

Data Type Action
Warranty items (names, dates, prices, notes) Deleted
Groups and sub-groups Deleted
Receipt photos and product images Deleted
Attached documents (PDFs, files) Deleted
Warranty claims and resolutions Deleted
Account profile and preferences Deleted

What Data Is Retained

Data Type Action
Nothing retained — All data is permanently deleted. No data is kept after processing your request.

Important: Account and data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please export your data from within the app (Settings > Data Management > Export Data) before requesting deletion if you wish to keep a copy.

In-App Data Deletion

You can also delete all local data directly within the app by going to Settings > Data Management > Delete All Data. This removes all items, groups, and photos from the app on your device. To also remove any cloud-stored data, please submit a deletion request via email as described above.

Questions?

If you have any questions about data deletion or your privacy, please contact us at mbj2easy@gmail.com.
