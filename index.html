MyWrntee

Warranty Tracker & Organizer

Never lose track of a warranty again. Organize, monitor, and protect all your product warranties in one place.

Available on iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Watch

Coming Soon on the App Store

Everything You Need

📷

Scan Receipts

Use your camera or photo library to scan receipts with Live Text recognition. Review extracted text and tap to assign store, date, price, and more.

📁

Organize Your Way

Create custom groups and sub-groups like Home > Kitchen or Office > Laptops. Add custom photos or colorful icons to each group.

⏰

Stay Ahead of Expirations

The dashboard shows your warranty timeline at a glance. See which warranties are expiring soon and your total protected value.

🖼️

Attach Photos

Save receipt photos and up to 9 product images alongside each warranty. Everything you need for a claim is right there.

☁️

Sync Across Devices

Enable iCloud sync to keep your warranties up to date on iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Watch. Your data stays in your personal iCloud.

📤

Export & Share

Export your warranty records as CSV or PDF with embedded images. Share a beautifully formatted warranty card via email or any app.

🔔

Expiry Reminders

Get push notifications before your warranties expire. Choose from 30, 14, 7, or 1 day reminders so you never miss a claim window.

📱

Home Screen Widgets

See your expiring warranties at a glance with small, medium, and large home screen widgets. Always stay informed without opening the app.

🗣️

Siri & Spotlight

Search your warranties from Spotlight or ask Siri to show expiring items. Quick access without even opening the app.

📍

Smart Location

Address autocomplete when entering purchase locations. Tap any saved location to open it directly in Apple Maps.

⌚

Apple Watch App

Check your warranties right from your wrist. View expiring items, browse by group, and see full warranty details. Requires iCloud Sync to be enabled.

📋

Tap to Assign

After scanning a receipt, review the extracted text and tap each line to assign it to the right field. You control what goes where.

♾️

Lifetime Warranties

Quickly set warranty periods with preset buttons including 1, 2, 3, and 5 year options — plus Lifetime for products that are covered forever.

🔄

Sort & Filter

Sort your items by name, purchase date, expiry date, or price. Filter by active or expired warranties to find what you need fast.

⚡

Quick Actions

Long-press the app icon to instantly add a new item or jump to expiring warranties. Get to what you need without even opening the app.

📊

Warranty Insights

See your average warranty length and cost per month of protection right on the dashboard. Understand the value of your warranty coverage at a glance.

🔒

Face ID & Touch ID Lock

Protect your warranty data with Face ID, Touch ID, or Optic ID. The app locks automatically when you leave and requires authentication to reopen.

🎨

Custom Accent Colors

Personalize your experience with 10 accent color options. Choose from Blue, Indigo, Purple, Pink, Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Teal, or Cyan in Settings.

📥

Batch Import

Add multiple warranty items at once after a big shopping trip. Fill in names, companies, and warranty periods in a streamlined list, then save them all with one tap.

👋

Guided Onboarding

New users get a beautiful 4-page walkthrough covering all key features and tips, plus 6 starter group templates including Home, Office, Personal Tech, Outdoor & Fitness, Family, and Auto & Vehicle.

📎

Document Attachments

Attach PDFs, emails, and other warranty documents alongside your photos. Preview any document with QuickLook right inside the app.

📝

Warranty Claim Tracking

Track warranty claims from start to finish. Log claim dates, reference numbers, status updates, and resolutions all in one place.

📦

Share & Import Groups

Export entire groups as .mywrntee files including all items, photos, receipts, documents, and claims. Share with family or friends and import everything in a single tap.

Designed for iPhone, iPad & Apple Watch

Built with SwiftUI featuring the latest Liquid Glass design.

Free with No Ads

MyWrntee is completely free with no advertisements and no tracking. Your data is yours and stays on your device. Optional tips are available to support development.
